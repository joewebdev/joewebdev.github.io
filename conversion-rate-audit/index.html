Conversion Rate Audit

Prepare before you optimize

“I want to increase the number of sales/leads I generate on my website”. This is statement that means your ready to take another step forward in improving your businesses online presence.

Before you can start doing tests to see what the best way to improve your conversion rate (the number of sale/leads per visitor), it’s a good idea to review the site to see if there are any bugs present, identify the type of experiments, and determine a baseline conversion rate.

Setting a benchmark

It's important to analyze the data you already have in order to know how much of an improvement you get from doing conversion rate optimization. That's why we start with reviewing past conversion rates of various key performance indicators (KPI) across your website. There's a lot of data and a lot of information to be found, if you know where to look. Fortunately, we do.

Get those pesky bugs out of the way

Bugs (a term used to indicate something is not showing/working as expected) on your website can get in the way of conversion rate optimization. During our audit, we'll identify any bugs that should be fixed before starting experimenting on your website.

What are your current KPIs?

Key performance indicators are vital to making the best out of conversion rate optimization. During the audit, we will identify areas that would be best suited to undergo experiments. If you have some existing KPIs, we can analyze those and, based on your websites historical data and related industry data, determine if those are worth investing time in.

How do I know if I need to have a Conversion Rate Audit?

If you look at your monthly revenue or your lead list and ask yourself some of the following questions or feel these concerns, then it’s time to reach out for a conversion rate audit.

We get a lot traffic, where are the sales/leads???

Getting a lot of traffic doesn’t always equate to getting a lot of sales. There are many factors involved in drawing a conclusion as to why. We know those factors and can get you answers.

Should we redesign this key page on our site?

Maybe you’re just looking to spruce up your website or a section or two with a new look. Before you do that, let us identify what you were achieving with the existing designs.

Customers are telling me they can't find the Call to action button...WHy?

Your customers might not realize what you want them to do on your website. Understanding your audience is a key part in our audit. Knowing the audience helps to improve the users experience and improves the chances of conversion on your website.
