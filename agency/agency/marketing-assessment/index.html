Marketing Assesment

There's always room to improve

“I’m happy with my marketing plan”. Ok, that’s a fair statement. But, what if you could make it even better? 

Utilizing search engine optimization, social media and email marketing is not a marketing plan. These are tools that allow you to reach the goals of your marketing plan. If you use any of those tools, but aren’t sure of what specifically you’re trying to accomplish, then it’s time to sit down and talk about it.

Search engine optimization

How do you know that ranking on page one for a given keyword or keyphrase is what will help bring more business? Are your customers using Google or Bing? Maybe. But, what if they aren't? You could be throwing money into SEO without seeing a return. Let's find out.

Paid Advertisements

If you're paying for advertisement on Facebook, Google Ads or any other web based platform, have you set goals for your return on investment (ROI)? Are you targeting the right demographics? Have you honed in on your converting demographics? Let's find out.

E-mail campaigns

E-mail campaigns are important, there's no doubt about that. But, there's a thin line between keeping in touch and being annoying. Are the e-mails your sending out relevant to each individual on your mailing list? Let's find out.
